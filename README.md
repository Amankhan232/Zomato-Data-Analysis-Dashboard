# Zomato-Data-Analysis-Dashboard
 ## Project Overview

The Zomato Analytics Dashboard is a Business Intelligence solution built using Power BI to analyze food delivery operations, restaurant performance, and customer behavior.This dashboard transforms raw order and review data into interactive insights that help stakeholders monitor KPIs and improve operational efficiency.

 ## Business Objectives

Monitor order and delivery performance

Track restaurant ratings and reviews

Analyze customer preferences

Identify revenue trends

Improve decision making using KPI metrics

## Dataset Used
- <a href="https://github.com/Amankhan232/Zomato-Data-Analysis-Dashboard/blob/main/Zomato.xlsx">Dataset</a>

## Questions(KPIs)
- How many total orders were placed during the selected time period?

- What is the total revenue generated and the Average Order Value (AOV)?

- How does order volume trend over time (daily/weekly/monthly)?

- What is the average delivery time across different cities, and which locations face delays?

- What is the overall customer satisfaction rating, and how are ratings distributed (1–5 scale)?

- Which restaurants generate the highest revenue and maintain strong ratings?

- Which cuisines are most popular based on order count and revenue contribution?

 ## Process
- Requirement Gathering
Defined business objectives and identified key KPIs from the BRD.

- Data Collection
Imported Orders, Restaurant, and Reviews datasets.

- Data Cleaning & Transformation
Handled missing values, standardized formats, and created calculated fields.

- Data Modeling
Established relationships between datasets for accurate analysis.

- KPI & DAX Implementation
Created measures for Total Orders, AOV, Delivery Time, Ratings, and Discount Utilization.

- Dashboard Design
Built interactive visualizations with filters and drill-down capabilities.

- Performance Optimization
Optimized data model and measures to ensure fast loading and scalability.

## Project Insights
-  Order Trends:
Order volume shows clear peak periods, indicating high demand during specific days and time ranges.

- Revenue Drivers:
A small group of top-performing restaurants contributes a significant portion of total revenue.

- Delivery Efficiency:
Certain cities experience higher average delivery times, which may impact customer satisfaction.

- Customer Satisfaction Pattern:
The majority of ratings fall in the 4–5 star range, indicating generally positive customer experience.

- Impact of Delivery Time on Ratings:
Orders with longer delivery times tend to receive relatively lower ratings.

## Final Conclusions
The Zomato Analytics Dashboard successfully transforms raw operational data into actionable business insights.

The analysis highlights key revenue driving restaurants, identifies delivery performance gaps, and reveals customer behavior trends. By tracking KPIs such as Total Orders, Average Delivery Time, AOV, and Customer Ratings, stakeholders can make data driven decisions to improve operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Overall, this dashboard demonstrates how Business Intelligence tools like Power BI can support strategic planning, performance monitoring, and business growth in the food delivery industry.
